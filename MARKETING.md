# Marketing Plan — Podcast Guest Pitch & Media Kit Generator

## Marketplace Listing Copy

**Title:** Podcast Guest Pitch & Media Kit Generator — Get Booked Without a PR Firm

**Tagline:** 4 prompts. Personalized pitch, media kit, follow-up sequence, repurposing kit. Everything a podcast host needs to say yes.

**Short Description (150 words):**
Getting booked on podcasts is the highest-leverage marketing move an expert can make — but most pitches get ignored. This skill generates a complete podcast guest pitching system: a personalized pitch email with 3 topic angles, a polished one-page media kit that hosts actually read, a 3-part professional follow-up sequence, and a 10-asset repurposing kit that turns one episode into a month of content.

No PR firm. No $2K/month retainer. Just paste your credentials, run the prompts, and send.

**Long Description (300 words):**
135 million Americans listen to podcasts every month. Every episode host is actively looking for quality guests. The bottleneck isn't demand — it's your pitch.

Most experts send the same generic email to 50 shows. Hosts delete it in 3 seconds. The ones who get booked have three things: a personalized pitch that references the specific show, a media kit that answers every question a host has before they ask it, and a professional follow-up sequence that closes the loop without burning the relationship.

This skill gives you all of that.

**Prompt 1** generates a personalized pitch email for any target podcast — 3 topic angle options (each with a working title and bullet takeaways), 3 subject line variants, proof points framed without bragging, and a soft CTA that respects the host's time.

**Prompt 2** builds your one-page media kit: bio in 3 lengths, 5 signature topic cards, audience fit framing, social proof block, technical specs, FAQ, and booking contact — formatted exactly how hosts want to receive it.

**Prompt 3** generates a 3-email follow-up sequence (Day 3 / Day 7 / Day 14) that adds new value at each touch and closes gracefully if there's no reply. No burning bridges. No being annoying. Just professional persistence.

**Prompt 4** creates 10 repurposing assets from one completed episode: LinkedIn post, Twitter thread, Instagram caption, email to your list, 60-second video script, blog intro, guest bio, quote card text, podcast directory description, and a media kit update with the new appearance.

One interview + Prompt 4 = one month of content. Podcast guesting becomes your most leveraged marketing channel.
