## How To Use

### Step 1 — Pitch Email (Prompt 1, free preview)
Run with your credentials and a specific podcast target. Get 3 topic angles + 3 subject lines.

### Step 2 — Media Kit (Prompt 2)
Run once. Save the output. Attach to every pitch you send.

### Step 3 — Follow-Ups (Prompt 3)
When hosts don't reply (most won't, right away). Personalize the Day 3 email and send.

### Step 4 — After the Interview (Prompt 4)
Run once. Post one asset per day for 10 days. Your one recording becomes a month of content.
